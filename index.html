The Story

Beyond
The Ring.

Karim Mayfield is San Francisco's own — a former professional boxing champion who rose from the Bay Area streets to become a world-ranked contender, then channeled that same championship drive into building something bigger than boxing.

As a fight promoter, Karim continues to shape the sport that shaped him. Through Soul Champ, his community initiative, he works directly with youth across the Bay Area — bringing the lessons of discipline, resilience, and self-belief that boxing instilled in him to the next generation.

As a producer of the Tubi film Inhumane and founder of the Bay Area lifestyle brand 415 Authentic, Karim has proven his vision extends far beyond any ring. One champion. Many rings.
